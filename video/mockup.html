About

I design products that work the way users expect — and businesses need.

I'm Anandu Sivan, an Interaction and UX Designer based in Glasgow, working with teams across the UK and internationally.

I'm not interested in making things pretty. I'm interested in making things work — and then making them feel effortless. That means starting with research, not assumptions.
